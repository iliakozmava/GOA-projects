here's a quick summary of the movie "Harry Potter"

Harry Potter is an orphaned boy who learns on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry where he makes close friends uncovers magical secrets and faces the dark wizard Lord Voldemort Across seven years he grows from a curious child into a brave young hero fighting to save the wizarding world
